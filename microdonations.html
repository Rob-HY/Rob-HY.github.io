Why Support Charities Through MicroDonations

More and more consumers and companies are becoming socially conscious. Supporting charitable causes signals your commitment to important issues and fosters customer loyalty. MicroDonations partners with vetted charities, ensuring your contributions are impactful. Through a successful pilot program with Billetto, DRC, and Sverige for UNHCR, MicroDonations raised over 200,000 euros for refugees in need.

Three Simple Ways to Support Charities

Customer Optional Donation

Give customers the option to donate a small amount during checkout.

Ecommerce Company Automatic Donation

Automatically donate 1% of your revenue to a chosen charity.

Match Customer Donations

Match your customers' donations up to a set amount for a larger impact.

How It Works

Step 1: Create an Account

Get started by creating an account to manage your charity contributions.

Step 2: Choose Your Implementation

Select from a Shopify, Stripe, or Wix plugin, or an SDK to integrate with your store.

Step 3: Select Charities

Pick from a list of trusted charities or suggest new ones.

Comprehensive Analytics and Reporting

We handle all the financials and send monthly reports to both you and the charity, keeping your donation efforts organized.
